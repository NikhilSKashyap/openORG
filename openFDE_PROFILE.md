# openFDE Protocol Profile

openFDE covers the customer mission from discovery through design, build,
verification, deployment, and measured handoff.

## Required Capture

- customer mission and success criteria
- current system, data location, quality, permissions, and sensitivity
- technical, business, security, cost, and timeline constraints
- architecture and the judgment about where AI should or should not be used
- alternatives, rationale, approval, and unresolved questions
- implementation plan, human and agent actions, and artifacts
- model, tool, skill, harness, MCP, and context-envelope versions
- cost, latency, corrections, and rejected recommendations

## Verification

Technical verification cites tests, evaluations, security checks, commits,
deployments, and independent review. Customer acceptance and production outcome
are separate records from technical success.

## Produces

`constraint`, `decision`, `work`, `artifact`, `verification`, `correction`, and
`outcome` records.
