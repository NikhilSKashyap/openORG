# openGTM Protocol Profile

openGTM helps GTM teams work in their existing CRM, email, meeting, support, and
analytics systems while preserving customer evidence and commercial outcomes.

## Required Capture

- account and stakeholder references without duplicating unnecessary PII
- source interaction and exact customer language
- pain, desired outcome, urgency, objections, and alternatives
- representative segment and confidence in the interpretation
- commitments, owners, deadlines, and next steps
- consent, classification, and retention policy

## Verification

Signals cite the transcript, email, CRM event, or other source. Commercial
commitments require an authorized human assertion. Outcomes cite CRM state,
contract events, adoption telemetry, or another governed measurement.

## Produces

`source`, `signal`, `claim`, `commitment`, `correction`, and `outcome` records.
