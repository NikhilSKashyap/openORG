# openPM Protocol Profile

openPM turns cross-functional signals into explicit product decisions and
measurable initiatives without erasing alternatives or disagreement.

## Required Capture

- problem and target user
- supporting and contradicting signals
- signal diversity and affected segment
- hypothesis, alternatives, rationale, scope, and non-goals
- decision owner, approvers, constraints, and dependencies
- baseline, target metric, measurement plan, and review date

## Verification

Decision approval is distinct from outcome verification. Product outcomes cite
experiments, analytics queries, customer evidence, or other governed sources.
Changed priorities append a superseding decision rather than rewriting history.

## Produces

`claim`, `constraint`, `decision`, `commitment`, `correction`, and `outcome`
records.
